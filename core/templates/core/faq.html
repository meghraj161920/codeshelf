# General Questions

What is CodeShelf?

CodeShelf is a premium marketplace platform where students, developers, and professionals can buy and sell real-world, production-ready coding projects and templates.

Who can use this platform?

Our platform is built for everyone! CS Students looking for project references, freelance developers needing a base template, and experienced engineers wanting to monetize their existing codebases.

Do the projects include databases?

Yes! The vast majority of our full-stack projects include exported database schemas (like .sql or sqlite files) and detailed setup instructions in the README.

# Account Management

How do I create an account?

You can easily register by clicking the 'Register' button in the navigation bar. You simply need to provide an email, a username, and select whether you want to be a Customer or a Seller.

Can I switch my role later?

Currently, roles are fixed upon registration to ensure security and proper dashboard routing. If you created a customer account but now wish to sell, please contact support to have your account upgraded.

# Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are supported?

We support all major payment methods including UPI, Credit/Debit cards, Net Banking, and popular mobile wallets through our secure payment gateway.

Can I get a refund if the code doesn't work?

Because our products are digital downloads, we generally do not offer refunds. However, if the zip file is corrupted or grossly misrepresents the product description, you can open a dispute ticket within 3 days of purchase.

# Seller Hub

How can I upload a project?

Once you are logged in as a Seller, navigate to your Dashboard and click "Upload Project". You will need to provide a title, description, zip file, and set your price.

How do I earn money?

You earn money every time a verified user purchases your uploaded projects. Your earnings accumulate in your seller wallet and are paid out automatically at the end of the month.
