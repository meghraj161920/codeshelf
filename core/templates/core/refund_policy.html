At CodeShelf, we aim to provide high-quality digital products. Please read our refund policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Products

All products sold on CodeShelf are digital downloads. Once a product is purchased and accessed, it cannot be returned physically.

2. No Refund Policy

Due to the nature of digital content, all sales are final and non-refundable. We do not offer refunds once the product has been downloaded or accessed.

3. Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • The product file is corrupted or not accessible
  • You were charged multiple times for the same product
  • The product does not match the description significantly
4. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, please contact us within 48 hours of purchase. Include your order details and reason for the request.

5. Processing Time

If your refund is approved, it will be processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.
